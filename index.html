A little more about me

I grew up in the foothills of the rocky mountains with its harsh weather, hard work, and all the difficulties that come along with living rurally. Because of this, I have learned that persistence, having a great attitude, and a hard work ethic can get you whatever you want in life. I graduated from the University of Wyoming with a BA in Business Economics whilst working a full-time job at a sign shop, was an intern for UW athletics marketing department, the fundraising chair of Alpha Kappa Psi, and was a 1st and 3rd place winner of their marketing strategy case competition. I studied abroad in Italy for 6 weeks and pitched a marketing plan and strategy for a local Italian business which was then implemented after my departure. After a year of experience in corporate financial planning, I found that my energy and passion for the business was much greater than these environments could allow for. After remembering my love for marketing work and the physical application of marketing through the sign industry, I realized I needed to apply my skills as an entrepreneur. Through my experience and connections with the sign industry, I leveraged an opportunity to expand Mondo’s digital marketing services and I absolutely love my work, our clients, and the digital-nomad flexibility Mondo allows for.

Oh, I also ❤️ to travel.
